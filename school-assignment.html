Mt.Pleasant, South Carolina

Biography:

Wando High School is a public high school within the Charleston County School District. Wando is the largest high school in the state of South Carolina based upon the enrollment of approximately 4,000 students each year. The school offers a wide range of AP, Dual Credit, Honors, and College Preparatory courses.

Teacher Overview

Mr. Tyler engages students minds through the wacky history that is European History. Students learn important historical figures as well as plenty of battles and wars. In his free time, Mr. Tyler hangs out with his son and wife.
